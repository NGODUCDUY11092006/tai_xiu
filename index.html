Game Rules

Tài Xỉu is a traditional Vietnamese dice game where players bet on the outcome of three dice.

Tài (Big):

  • Total sum of dice is between 11 and 18

Xỉu (Small):

  • Total sum of dice is between 3 and 10

How to play:

  1. Choose your bet amount
  2. Select either Tài (Big) or Xỉu (Small)
  3. Click "Roll Dice" to see the outcome
  4. If you win, you get double your bet amount
